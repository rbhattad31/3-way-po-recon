How the posting engine uses this data: Vendors are matched by exact code, then alias, then fuzzy name. Items follow the same chain. Tax codes are matched by invoice-extracted tax percentage and country. Cost centers are matched from the invoice cost-center field. Open PO lines are cross-checked during the duplicate-invoice stage. All lookups first try the live ERP connector (if configured), then fall back to these imported tables. Manage ERP connectors.
Recon agent PO lookup — two-tier resolution: When the reconciliation agent looks up a PO it searches Tier 1 (transactional PurchaseOrder records loaded into the recon system) first, then falls back to Tier 2 (the Open POs tab above — imported ERP reference snapshot, confidence 0.75). A Tier-2 hit means the PO exists in the ERP but has not been loaded into the recon system as a full document; GRN linkage and line-level tax detail will be unavailable. Load the PO into the system to upgrade it to Tier 1.
